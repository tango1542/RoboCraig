Welcome to RoboCopy’s documentation!¶

Robocopy will do automatic searches of saved searches for Craigslist items

  • Enter some search details for an item in Minneapolis Craigslist

  • Your searches will be saved

  • Robocraig will automatically search for Craigslist results with those details

  • You can update your searches with different filters, and you can delete your searches
